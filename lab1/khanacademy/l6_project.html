What is Milky Cake?

A vanilla cake is a baked good made with flour, butter, eggs, leavening agents like baking soda or baking powder, and flavored with vanilla bean pods or extract. A common choice for birthday cake, this easily customizable dessert pairs perfectly with many icings and frostings, from lemon-flavored to classic American buttercream.
