Turkish manty

  • Time: 120 min
  • Serves: smth
Ingredients Quantity
dough 800g
ground beef 200g
onin 1 piece
salt 0.5 tsp

Step 1: Prepare the filling. In a medium bowl, combine ground meat, finely chopped onions, parsley, salt and pepper. Put it aside..

Step 2: Sift a little flour on the counter. Grab the dough ball and roll it out, not very thin, about 2mm thick. Cut it first into strips, then into small squares. Place about Â½ teaspoon filling on each square.

Step 3: Heat 4 cups of water in a pot, add in a pinch of salt and a drop of oil. Bring it to boil. Add in the dumplings and let it simmer until tender, for 10-12 minutes.

Step 4: For the butter and oil sauce, melt butter and add in olive oil. Add in dried mint and red pepper flakes or paprika, stir. Take it after about 20 seconds. .

Lemon Crinkle Cake2

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: smth
Ingredients Quantity
lemon cake mix 15.25 ounce
eggs 2
olive oil 1/3 cup
lemon juice 1 tablespoon
powdered sugare 1/3 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375F/190C. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2: In a mixing bowl, whisk together cake mix, eggs, oil, lemon juice and lemon zest.

Step 3: Chill the dough for 30 minutes (optional).

Step 4: Make balls and drop them into a bowl of powderd sugar. Coat all sides with powderd sugar..

Step 5: Place them on the prepared baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes.

Step 6: Let them cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes and then transfer to a cooling rack.
